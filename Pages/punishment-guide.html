Server

⮞ Spam

Spam disrupts the flow of conversation and can make it difficult for others to communicate effectively. Please refrain from sending the same message multiple times or overwhelming the chat with excessive messages. Whether it’s repeating phrases, flooding the chat with random characters, or posting unnecessary links, all forms of spam are prohibited.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 15m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 30m Mute

⮞ Inappropriate Language

We want our server to be a welcoming place for players of all ages. Using inappropriate language, including mass swearing, insults, or any form of offensive speech, is not allowed. This rule helps maintain a friendly and respectful environment where everyone feels comfortable.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 15m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 30m Mute

⮞ Disrespect

Respect is crucial in our community. Disrespectful behaviour, such as name-calling, harassment, or belittling others, is not tolerated. This includes any form of bullying or unfair treatment towards other players or staff members.

  • Violation #1 - 15m Mute
  • Violation #2 - 30m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 1h Mute

⮞ Media Advertisements

Advertising unrelated media can clutter the chat and distract from the game. Please do not use the server to promote other games, websites, or products. Our server is meant for playing and enjoying Minecraft together, not for external promotions.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 30m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 1h Mute

⮞ Server Advertisements

Promoting other Minecraft servers on our server is not allowed. When players advertise other servers, it can disrupt the community and lead to fragmentation. To keep our server thriving and unified, please avoid sharing information about or encouraging others to join different servers.

  • Violation #1 - Perm Mute
  • Violation #2 - Perm Ban

⮞ Discrimination

Our server is a place where everyone should feel safe and accepted. Discrimination in any form is strictly prohibited. This includes any actions or speech that demean or unfairly target individuals or groups based on their characteristics.

  • Violation #1 - 3d Mute
  • Violation #2 - 7d Mute
  • Violation #3 - 30d Mute
  • Violation #4 - Perm Mute

⮞ Harmful Intent

Intentionally trying to cause mental or emotional harm to others is strictly prohibited. This includes making threats, encouraging harmful behaviours such as suicide, or engaging in any actions that could distress or hurt other players emotionally.

  • Violation #1 - 3d Mute
  • Violation #2 - 7d Mute
  • Violation #3 - 30d Mute
  • Violation #4 - Perm Mute

⮞ Security Risk

Protecting the safety and security of our server and its players is a top priority. Posting malicious links, such as those leading to viruses, phishing sites, or other harmful content, is strictly forbidden. These actions can compromise the security of players' personal information and devices. Always be cautious about clicking on links, and ensure that anything you share is safe and trustworthy.

This punishment may also be used when players are too unruly for our community and so can be permanently removed from all platforms.

  • Violation #1 - Perm ban

⮞ IRL Soliciting

Soliciting real-life goods, services, or personal information is not allowed on our server. This includes asking for donations, selling items for real currencies, trading items across servers/platforms, or requesting personal details from other players.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 30d Ban
  • Violation #1 - Perm ban

⮞ Encouraging Misuse

Encouraging others to break server rules or misuse features undermines the integrity of our community. Whether it’s telling someone to exploit a bug or suggesting they spam the chat, promoting rule-breaking behaviour is not allowed.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 30m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 1h Mute

⮞ DDOS Threats

DDOS threats are taken very seriously. These threats involve attempting to overload the server or players’ connections to cause disruption. Such actions can cause significant harm and are illegal.

  • Violation #1 - Perm Ban

⮞ DOX Threats

DOX threats involve the sharing of someone’s personal information, such as their address or phone number, without permission. This is a severe violation of privacy and is strictly forbidden.

  • Violation #1 - Perm Ban

⮞ Inappropriate Builds

Inappropriate builds include but are not limited to structures that depict hate symbols, explicit content, or anything designed to offend or disrupt other players.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 1d ban
  • Violation #3 - 3d Ban

⮞ Inappropriate Items

Inappropriate Items are item names that depict hate symbols, explicit content, or anything designed to offend or disrupt other players.

  • Violation #1 - Warn & take item to rename and return
  • Violation #2 - 1d Ban and Item gets destroyed
  • Violation #3 - 3d Ban and Item gets destroyed

⮞ Inappropriate Minecraft Profile

Players are expected to maintain profiles that are respectful and appropriate for all ages. This includes avoiding usernames, skins, and capes that contain offensive language or imagery.

  • Violation #1 - Perm Ban (appealable)
  • Violation #2 - Perm Ban (unappealable)

⮞ Griefing

Griefing is the act of intentionally destroying, altering, or otherwise damaging another player’s structures or items without permission. This can ruin the enjoyment of the game for other players by destroying their hard work and investments. Our server aims to foster a cooperative and friendly community where players can build and explore without fear of their creations being vandalized.

  • Violation #1 - 1d Ban
  • Violation #2 - 3d Ban
  • Violation #3 - 7d Ban
  • Violation #4 - Perm Ban

⮞ TP Trapping

TP trapping involves using the teleportation (TP) function to lure players into traps, leading to the potential loss of items or lives. This practice disrupts fair play and trust among players. Please ensure you only TP to players you can trust and record in case you aren't sure so that you can help staff. Do not set up any type of trapping devices that can lure players into being teleported and stuck, harmed, or killed.

  • Violation #1 - 1d Ban
  • Violation #2 - 3d Ban
  • Violation #3 - 7d Ban
  • Violation #4 - Perm Ban

⮞ Claim Disruption

Claim Disruption involves claiming areas of someone else's claim to be an annoyance, attempting to block their claim by claiming directly around it, or attempting to use another player's claim to cause disruption (for example, sitting on their claim to steal items or get in the way of them attempting to play and enjoy the server).

  • Violation #1 - 1d Ban
  • Violation #2 - 3d Ban
  • Violation #3 - 7d Ban

⮞ Scamming

Scamming involves deceiving other players to steal items, currency, or services through false promises or trades. This undermines the trust and integrity of the server’s economy and player interactions. All trades and agreements between players should be conducted honestly and fairly.

  • Violation #1 - 3d Ban
  • Violation #2 - 7d Ban
  • Violation #3 - 30d Ban
  • Violation #4 - Perm Ban

⮞ Unfair Advantage

Unfair advantage refers to the use of hacks, mods, or any third-party software that gives players an unfair advantage over others. Using hacks, mods, or other tools to gain an unfair advantage disrupts the balance of the game and can significantly impact other players' experiences.

  • Violation #1 - 7d Ban
  • Violation #2 - 30d Ban
  • Violation #3 - Perm Ban

⮞ Abusing Exploits

Abusing exploits involves the use of in-game bugs or glitches to gain an unfair advantage or disrupt gameplay. Exploits can range from duplication glitches to bugs that allow players to bypass game mechanics. While discovering a bug is sometimes inevitable, intentionally abusing it to benefit oneself or disrupt the game for others is not acceptable. Players are encouraged to report exploits rather than abuse them.

  • Violation #1 - Perm Ban

⮞ Mute Evasion

Mute evasion involves attempting to bypass a mute issued by server staff by using alternate accounts or other means. We ask that if you disagree with a punishment, you make a ticket to appeal it rather than attempting to evade it.

  • Violation #1 - Mimics the timeframe of the main account's punishment
  • Violation #2 - Perm Mute

⮞ Ban Evasion

Ban evasion refers to creating new accounts or using other methods to bypass a ban issued by server staff. We ask that you make a ticket to appeal a punishment rather than attempting to evade it if you disagree with it.

  • Violation #1 - Mimics the timeframe of the main account's punishment
  • Violation #2 - Perm Ban
