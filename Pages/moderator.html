Moderator Role

Requirements

As a moderator you have more responsabilities than a helper. You have now access to see automod notifications in chat about xray and chat offences.

Permissions

After you become moderator you gain access to a lot more commands such as:

/warn /mute /ban /history /vanish /tp

Advice

  • Scroll thru the #denied-punishments channel to understand the line between what we punish and we dont punish. This is very useful if you're unsure of spam or inapp language offences.
  • Engage in community as often as possible. Building trust between you and the playerbase improves you as a staff member.

Notes

For the time being helpers cannot mute for longer than 1 Day so if the punishment requires longer you have to go to #staff-support and put all information there.
