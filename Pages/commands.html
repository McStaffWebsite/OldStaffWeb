Server

⮞ /warn

The /warn command is for many first offence punishments. The attributes are important to use the command properly.

  • /warn [IGN] - Is for the specific ign to warn. Its a requiered attribute to use the command. For example /warn _DragonRush
  • /warn ign [Reason] - Is important for the offender and other staff to understand why you punished. It is required to put the reason you warned for and what number offense it was. For example /warn _DragonRush spam #1

⮞ /mute

The /mute command is for many first offence punishments. The attributes are important to use the command properly.

  • /mute [IGN] - Is for the specific ign to mute. Its a requiered attribute to use the command.
  • /mute ign [Timestamp] - Is for specifying the length of the mute for example /mute _DragonRush 7d
  • /mute ign timestamp [Reason] - Is important for the offender and other staff to understand why you punished. It is required to put the reason you muted for and what number offense it was. For example /mute _DragonRush 7d unfair advantage #1

⮞ /ban

  • /ban ign [Timestamp] - Is for specifying the length of the ban for example /ban _DragonRush 7d
  • /ban ign timestamp [Reason] - Is important for the offender and other staff to understand why you punished. It is required to put the reason you banned for and what number offense it was. For example /ban _DragonRush 7d unfair advantage #1

⮞ /history

The /history command is for seeing the past punishments of a player.

  • /history [IGN] - Ign is for the player u want to see the history off.
  • /history ign [List amount] - Is for listing a specific amount from the history. /history _DragonRush 30

⮞ Spam

Spam disrupts the flow of conversation and can make it difficult for others to communicate effectively. Please refrain from sending the same message multiple times or overwhelming the chat with excessive messages. Whether it’s repeating phrases, flooding the chat with random characters, or posting unnecessary links, all forms of spam are prohibited.

  • Violation #1 - Warn
  • Violation #2 - 15m Mute
  • Violation #3 - 30m Mute
