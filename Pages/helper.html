Helper Role

Information

Welcome to the staff team! When you first get your rank ingame you might notice that all the staff are talking in a private chat. To do so you have to use the /sc command. It will send a message only staff can read

Requirements

As a helper you are expected to learn within your role and assist in chat at all times, meaning you will need to be able to answer questions, read and monitor chat, respond to staff chat and help with reports against players. As well as for channels you're going to use on the discord always make sure to check pins for any important information and or templates.

Permissions

As a helper you have a limited amount of permissions. You have:

Server perms

/warn /mute /history /stafflist /sc

Discord perms

Any additional commands can be found in Staff permissions doc

Advice

  • Scroll thru the #denied-punishments channel to understand the line between what we punish and we dont punish. This is very useful if you're unsure of spam or inapp language offences.
  • Engage in community as often as possible. Building trust between you and the playerbase improves you as a staff member.

Notes

For the time being helpers cannot mute for longer than 1 Day so if the punishment requires longer you have to go to #staff-support and put all information there.
